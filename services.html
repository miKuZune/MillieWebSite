Amelia offers multiple design services to suit any creative project. If you don’t see something here that is right for your project, then please get in touch. Original Artwork & Illustration

Whether it be an original repeat pattern design, placement artwork or illustrations, Amelia would love to talk more about your project. This service includes a discussion about the design direction and inspiration, treatment piece, finished artwork and digital cleanup. Amelias natural painterly style leans more towards the Arts and Crafts movement and therefore her strength is in hand painting and printing. If this sounds like what your project needs, then please get in touch.

Design Direction boards

One of the most important stages of any creative project is outlining the design direction, aesthetic and mood. If this is something you are looking for, Amelia would love to help. She will enthusiastically translate your discussion and description of your project into a beautifully clear aesthetic presentations, telling the visual story. Or if trends are what you are looking for, Amelia can pull design direction boards together focussed on trends too.

Artwork Retouching

Taking a physical artwork or sample and transforming it into a digital file can be fiddly. But Amelia makes it easy. With her extensive experience in Photoshop, she can clean up and amend artworks, create fluid repeat pattern layouts, transform fabric samples into digital and make colour amends.

Lookbooks, Layouts & Web Design

Strong visual presentation is vital for any brand or creative looking to have a professional digital profile. That’s where Amelias acute eye for detail and infinite perfectionism can really help. With experience in successful lookbook design, she would be thrilled to help with the finishing touches to your project. If layouts for presentations are what you are after, then look no further. As well as this, Amelia can help with Web Design too! Not only the design but the programming as well, due to her industry connections. If any of these sound like what you need, then Get in touch via email
