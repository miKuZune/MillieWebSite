ABOUT

Meet the designer

My name is Amelia (or Millie) Farley. I am a Freelance Print Designer and Illustrator based in Hastings, East Sussex.

Growing up submerged in the winding country lanes of East Sussex, I quickly harnessed a passion for exploring the natural world and wanting to tell its stories through artwork. Inspired by my surroundings and the timeless ethos of the Arts and Crafts movement, I developed a passion for creating meticulous print designs and illustrations that exude individuality and sensitivity.

Having graduated from Nottingham Trent University with a First Class Degree in Textile Design, I was thrilled to go on to win the Habitat Future Design Award at New Designers in 2023. Diving straight into my career following New Designers, I completed work placements at brands such as Sanderson Design Group and Habitat. Here I acquired essential industry skills, developed my attentive eye for detail and gained confidence in answering a versatile range of design briefs.

When I’m not painting, flicking through books and magazines for inspiration or planning new design projects, you can likely find me out exploring the local areas with our family dog, Bonnie. I feel extremely lucky to live in such an inspiring part of the country. Surrounded by beaches, rolling countryside and quaint villages, I never struggle for inspiration, weather that be scenes of nature or discovering what other local creatives are getting up to.

Underpinning my enthusiasm for artwork and design, is a desire to create designs that bring joy and a curious individuality into peoples’ lives through colour and pattern. Stemming from my fondness of the the Arts and Crafts movement, I truly enjoy using traditional techniques, like block printing and manual repeat tiles, to ground my practice in heritage and stylised details. Combining this philosophy with my experienced digital practice allows me to explore design briefs in a fluid and fresh way. Alongside an acute awareness of the industry, I truly recognise the importance of creating designs that tap into consumers passions, going far beyond trends. I believe in creating designs that have been meticulously considered and are designed with purpose, passion and creativity at their core.
